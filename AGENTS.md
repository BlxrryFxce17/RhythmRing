## ⚠️ IMPORTANT RULES FOR ALL AGENTS

**IF YOU CANNOT FULFILL THE USER REQUEST, DO NOT APPROXIMATE.**

1.  **If you are missing required information:**
    *   Ask the user for clarification.
    *   Do NOT guess or make up data.

2.  **If you are unsure about implementation details:**
    *   State your uncertainty clearly.
    *   Suggest the user consult documentation or a senior developer.
    *   Do NOT provide incorrect or misleading information.

3.  **If you cannot perform a requested action (e.g., "apply global changes", "install packages"):**
    *   Explain your limitations honestly.
    *   Provide step-by-step instructions for the user to complete the task manually.
    *   Offer to review the user's manual implementation.

**NEVER PROVIDE FALSE INFORMATION OR MISLEADING CONFIRMATION.**
